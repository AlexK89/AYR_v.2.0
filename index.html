How it Works

01 Set your departure and destination cities. Then select the date(s) you wish to travel and the number of seats you need

02 If there is an existing flight that fits your schedule, jump on that one. If not, create your own flight, subject to availability

03 If you’ve opted to create your own flight, set a departure time that works best for you. Our handy app will help you decide which departure times will get you the best cost

04 Purchase your seat(s), receive our digital boarding pass, and get ready for the best flying experience you’ve ever had!

Our Business Model

Our goal at AYR is to provide the best possible value to busy, time-sensitive travelers. We do this by employing a business model built on three fundamental principles

Low Cost Carrier Model

We operate only one type of aircraft, the Pilatus PC-24, and only fly out of regional, conveniently located airports. This allows us to generate cost savings that we pass on to our customers!

Flexible Sharing Platform

We understand that your time is valuable. That's why, at AYR, not only do we allow the customer to set the departure time, we also provide per-seat charter options. Set your departure time, pay for only the seats you need, and leave the rest to us!

Dynamic Pricing Model

Simply put, we charge based on the demand we're likely to receive for a requested flight. With help from our handy app, you can see the best times to schedule your flight to get you the best possible quote. We pride ourselves on transparent pricing!
